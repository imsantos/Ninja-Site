FRIEND-NINJA

 Hey there Ninja! It's up to you to become a master among your friends! Swipe your finger across the screen to fight with your friends like a true ninja warrior! Be careful and don't slash yourself...that will cost you lives!

 Challenge your friends on facebook to a combat and win the duel slashing them how many times and faster as you can! Let's see who's the best!

 Outfit yourself before the fight and collect bombs, explosives and lives to improve your performance as a ninja in the game!

 This slice-em-up social game is the first launch from Codangel Game-maniacs division. It is also a new level on social games establishing a hierarchy on every circle of friends.
